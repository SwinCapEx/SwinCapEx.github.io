Shark Tank recipients

Teams from COS and EAT units had the opportunity to participate in a Shark Tank event and obtain funding to further develop their projects.

  • UG_EAT_84 BeSide- Safer Together

    Students: Shushmita Barua, Md Sarowar Hossain, Mahbuba Jerin, Namera Nayat, Tafriha Tahsin

    Supervisor: Naveed Ali

    Client: John Joyner, MP Outcomes

    BeSide- Safer Together is more than a safety app, it’s a movement to make every journey safe and trusted. Born from real fears of traveling alone, and from the growing reality that Melbourne no longer feels secure with rising assaults and street crimes making even daylight walks unsettling; BeSide transforms fear into empowerment. It’s a consent-driven safety app that connects verified users nearby to walk or travel together with confidence. With live GPS tracking, ID verification, and instant emergency alerts, BeSide builds safety through mutual trust. It’s not just technology; it’s hope, connection, and protection in one. BeSide- safer together, always.

  • UG_GAM_05 Neo-Phalem

    Students: Sam Briggs, Thomas Crow, Avery Flannery, Hamish Luke, Myn Ma, Raza Syed, Kevin Trevascus

    Supervisor: Q Tien Pham

    A Roleplaying game taking place in hell where you play as a fallen angel, in the game there is both turnbased and realtime combat, the player is able to use a range of different abilities to fight both demons and angels. The game's demo is targetted for a Windows and Linux releases with support for players to use a keyboard and mouse or a controller. In the demo, you will rescue your character's handler from a boss enemy while fighting demons and angelic constructs all while learning the mechanics of the game.
