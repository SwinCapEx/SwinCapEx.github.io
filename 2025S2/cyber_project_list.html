ICT30018 ICT Project B

This is a project-based unit in which students work individually, or in teams if the project is a large industry project, to provide students with the skills to generate an ICT solution to solve one or more industry challenges specifically designed and oriented towards ICT. In 2025, this cohort is exclusively working on innovative cybersecurity problems.
